About Us

What is BuildBillEase?

BuildBillEase is the newest innovation of the Local Government Unit of Caloocan to improve the application of Building Permits and Certificates of Occupancy. It aims to reduce processing time and provide a one-stop online experience while minimizing direct interaction with government offices.
